# 06 — Demo Video Script (2–5 minutes)

> The demo is a required deliverable and one of your biggest scoring levers. Keep it tight. The single most important thing: **show the system refusing when it doesn't know.** That one moment beats any feature.

## Structure (aim for ~3 minutes)

### 0:00–0:30 — The hook (your real story)
Speak as yourself, on camera or voiceover:
> "When I came to the US as an international student, just opening a bank account meant calling five different people to find out the documents and eligibility rules. Google sent me in circles, and when I asked an AI chatbot, it confidently gave me a wrong answer — because it didn't understand my visa situation. Every international student hits this same wall. So we built Clarity."

*This 30 seconds wins Problem Understanding. Do not skip or shorten it.*

### 0:30–1:00 — What it is (one clear sentence + the triad)
> "Clarity gives you plain-language answers to real-world rules — from official sources only, with the source shown on every answer, and an honest hand-off to a real person when the stakes are high. It is built so it can't make things up."

Show the home screen with the three journeys and the founder's one-line story.

### 1:00–2:00 — The perfect answer (live)
- Pick **Journey A — bank account.** Answer the short intake questions.
- Show the result: a clean ordered checklist — steps, documents, official links — with a visible **source name + "last verified" date** on the answer.
- Say: "Every fact here traces to an official source. Nothing is invented."

### 2:00–2:40 — THE WINNING MOMENT (the refusal)
- Ask something out of scope or unknown on purpose.
- Show the system respond honestly: *"I don't have a verified answer for that"* → and route to the right human contact.
- Say: "A system that knows its limits is more trustworthy than one that pretends to know everything. When it isn't sure — or when the question affects your visa status — it hands you to the right person instead of guessing."

*This 40 seconds wins Responsible AI and AI Reasoning.*

### 2:40–3:00 — Impact + close
> "Before Clarity: hours of calls, conflicting answers, and a chatbot that hallucinated. After: one conversation, a sourced checklist, and the right human for the hard part. We started with international students in Texas because we live this problem — but the same engine works for any person, place, or program."

Optional final frame: a roadmap line — "Next: more countries, more languages, more journeys."

## Filming tips
- Screen-record the live app; voiceover on top. Real, working > flashy.
- Practice the two key moments (perfect answer + refusal) until they run clean.
- Show the "last verified" badge clearly on screen — most teams won't have it.
- Keep it under 5 minutes; ~3 is ideal.

## The two moments to rehearse until flawless
1. **A hard question answered perfectly, with a citation and date.**
2. **An unknown question correctly refused and escalated.**

If only those two land cleanly, you've demonstrated the whole thesis.
